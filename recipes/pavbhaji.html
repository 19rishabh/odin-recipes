Pav Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a quintessential dish from the bustling streets of Mumbai, Maharashtra, but its popularity has transcended regional boundaries, making it a beloved dish all across India. The name 'Pav Bhaji' is derived from two Marathi words: 'Pav', which means a small loaf of bread, and 'Bhaji', which means vegetable.To eat Pav Bhaji, you tear off a piece of the Pav and use it to scoop up the Bhaji.Each bite is a delightful combination of the soft Pav, the spicy Bhaji, and the melted butter.

Whether it's for a quick snack or a full meal, Pav Bhaji is more than just food -it's comfort in a plate. The warm Pav soaked in butter, combined with the hearty Bhaji,creates an explosion of flavors that's incredibly satisfying. It's no wonder that Pav Bhaji has won over the hearts of food lovers everywhere.
